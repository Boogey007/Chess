Piece Movement

KING

The King can move in any direction one square at a time.

QUEEN

The Queen can move in any straight line forward, backward, left, right, or diagonal continuously.

ROOK

The Rook can move in any straight line forward, backward, left, right continuously.

KNIGHT

The knight can move in a straight L shape that goes over two squares then left or right once and vice versa

BISHOP

The Bishop can move in a continuous diagonal line while remaining on the same color.

PAWN

The pawn can move forward one space at a time and diagnonal to capture.
